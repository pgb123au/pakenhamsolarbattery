## Long description

> Pakenham Solar & Battery is the Cardinia Shire's CEC-accredited solar panel and battery installer. We design, install and service solar PV, home batteries, EV chargers and solar repairs across the Pakenham growth corridor (Pakenham, Officer, Cardinia Lakes, Lakeside, Pakenham East) and the wider Cardinia Shire (Heritage Springs, Beaconsfield, Nar Nar Goon, Pakenham Upper, Beaconsfield Upper, Gembrook, Maryknoll).
>
> Five services: solar panel installation (Tier-1 Trina, Jinko, REC panels with Fronius or Sungrow inverters, ~$5,500–$14,000 after STCs depending on size); battery storage (Tesla Powerwall 3, Sungrow and BYD, with the federal Cheaper Home Batteries rebate applied — around $252 per usable kWh in 2026); combined solar + battery systems (one hybrid design that stacks the battery rebate, Solar Victoria and STCs, ~$17,000–$19,000 net for a 10kW + 10kWh setup); EV charger installation (Tesla, Zappi, Wallbox integrated with your solar so you charge on free daytime sun); and solar repairs & servicing (inverter faults, underperforming systems, isolator and meter issues — any brand, even if your original installer is gone).
>
> Rebate-ready. We are a Clean Energy Council (CEC) accredited installer and designer — the requirement to claim STCs and the federal battery rebate — and we apply every entitlement as an upfront discount. We install to AS/NZS 5033 (PV array installation), AS/NZS 4777 (grid connection of inverters), AS/NZS 5139 (battery safety) and AS/NZS 3000 (Wiring Rules).
>
> United Energy (DNSP) connection handled. Pakenham and the Cardinia Shire are in the United Energy network, so every grid-connected system needs DNSP pre-approval and AS/NZS 4777 export design — we lodge the pre-approval and the connection paperwork as part of the job. ABN 91 664 546 061.
>
> Free, no-obligation solar & battery quotes across the Cardinia Shire. We check your bills, roof and meter, confirm your rebates, and size a system honestly.
>
> Call (03) 9003 0108 Monday to Saturday 7am–6pm, or visit pakenhamsolarbattery.com.au.

## Process

1. Tier 1 all in one sitting (~30 min) — same NAP.
2. After 24h, Google `"Pakenham Solar & Battery" -site:pakenhamsolarbattery.com.au` to confirm indexing.
3. Free upgrades → makes link do-follow.
4. Reword first paragraph each listing — avoid duplicate-content flag.
5. Track in spreadsheet: directory, profile URL, login, do-follow status, date.
